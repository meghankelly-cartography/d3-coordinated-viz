Global Immunizations: A UN millenium goal unmet?

UN Millenium Goals

In 2000, world UN leaders met and established 8 millenium goals that aimed to eradicate poverty, increase health standards, and promote education and gender equality. The fourth goal addresses children mortality and specifically targets the reduction of child morality rate by two thirds between 1990 and 2015. One strategy to accomplish this goal to expand immunization programs around the world.
